# Privacy Policy for ClemenTime

**Last Updated: July 24, 2026**

ClemenTime ("the App") is a schedule management tool.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
The App does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). All schedule data, notes, and attachments are stored locally on your device.

## 2. Permissions
- **Internet Access**: Required to import schedules from the online repository (GitHub). No user data is uploaded during this process.
- **Storage/File Access**: Required to link and view local file attachments and to export/import schedule backups.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The app uses Google Fonts and may fetch schedule data from GitHub. These services have their own privacy policies.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions, please contact the developer via GitHub.
